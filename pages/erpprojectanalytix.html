Client Analytix Lanka (Pvt) Ltd

Year : Live since 2024 and still operational

Purpose: The ERP System was developed to address the client's comprehensive business needs, focusing on streamlining various operational processes. It integrates both internal and external functionalities, making it a versatile solution for managing customer interactions, financial processes, and company registrations

Main Features

  1. Core Functionalities
    • Customer Registration : Onboarding new customers efficiently
    • Task Management : Creation and assignment of tasks within the system
    • Payment Handling : Updating payment statuses, managing payment collections, and handling outstanding balances
    • Proposal Creation : Streamlined process for generating and managing business proposals
    • Credit Reports : Generation of credit reports based on financial activities
  2. Company Registration Process
    • Supports internal company registration workflows and customer registration for external services
  3. TIN Registration
    • Facilitates both external and internal processes for registering Tax Identification Numbers
  4. Customer Portal
    • An external portal provided for customers to monitor their tasks, view upcoming tax payments, and access the history of their tax-related activities

Technology Stack

  • Backend: ASP.NET MVC, C#
  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • Database: SQL Server
